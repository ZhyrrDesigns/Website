Commission Pipeline

Want a world built specifically for your server, SMP, or friend group? Here’s how we go from idea → playable map, with clear steps, frequent updates, and a final result that matches what you imagine.

1
Idea & Direction

We talk about your vision and I ask the important questions: map size vs. player count, the vibe you want, biome spacing (some biomes work better farther apart), progression, landmarks, and any “must-have” features. I’ll share honest suggestions, but the goal is always the same: build what you envision.

2
Planning & Downpayment

Once the plan is locked in, I ask for a 50% downpayment to secure the slot and protect my time. After that, I start building.

3
Build Phase (Updates & Feedback)

I’ll send frequent progress updates and screenshots so you can react early, what you love, what you want adjusted, and what should be pushed further. This keeps us aligned and prevents surprises at the end.

4
Final Preview, Delivery & Small Tweaks

When the world is ready, I send in-game screenshots as a final preview. You complete the remaining 50%, and I deliver the map. After you test it, you can request small finishing touches so everything feels perfect.

FAQ

How do you create your terrains?

I create my terrains using my own custom brush and heightmap bundle, built over years of worldbuilding. These brushes let me shape mountains, coastlines, oceans, and biomes quickly while keeping everything natural and consistent across the map.

Create your own world →
How big are the worlds?

Most of my maps are massive handcrafted worlds, starting at 2k (2048 x 2048) and going up to 10k (9984 x 9984). These worlds are many times larger than standard custom maps and are designed to support long-term survival and exploration.

Do the maps blend into vanilla terrain?

On Java Edition, the handcrafted world blends seamlessly into a normal vanilla Minecraft seed, allowing infinite exploration beyond the custom area. On Bedrock, the custom world has a clear boundary due to technical limitations, but vanilla terrain continues beyond it.

Do structures have custom loot?

Yes. All structures use custom builds and randomized loot tables inspired by vanilla Minecraft. You will never find identical chests, and exploration is always rewarded.

Are animals and villagers included?

Animals spawn naturally in their correct biomes, just like vanilla. Villagers spawn in villages using spawners, so villages never run out of villagers during survival gameplay.

Can I use these maps for servers or content creation?

Absolutely. You are free to use my maps for servers, YouTube videos, survival with friends, or personal projects. Selling or redistributing the maps themselves is not allowed.
